permute - permute the dimensions of an array

Calling Sequence

y=permute(x,dims)

Parameters

  • dims: a scalar or a vector of positive reals.
  • x: a scalar, a vector, a matrix or a mutlti-array.

Description

y=permute(x,dims) :

Examples

//example 1:
x=[1 2 3;4 5 6];
y=permute(x,[2 1]);

//example 2:
x=matrix(1:12,[2,3,2]);
y=permute(x,[3 1 2]);
