eigenspace - maxplus eigenelements

Calling Sequence

[v,l]=eigenspace(A)

Parameters

  • A : maxplus full square matrix
  • v : eigenspace basis
  • l : eigenvalue

Description

Maxplus eigenvalues and basis of the corresponding eigenspace for a maxplus matrix

Examples

[v,l]=eigenspace(#(1))
a=#([1,2;3,4]);[v,l]=eigenspace(a)
a*v==l*v
a=#([1,0;0,1]); [v,l]=eigenspace(a)
a*v==l*v
