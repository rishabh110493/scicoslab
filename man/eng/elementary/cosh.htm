cosh - hyperbolic cosine

Calling Sequence

[t]=cosh(x)

Parameters

  • x,t: real or complex vectors/matrices

Description

The elements of t are the hyperbolic cosine of the corresponding entries of vector x.

Examples


x=[0,1,%i]
acosh(cosh(x))
